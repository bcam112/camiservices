Full Session Lifecycle

Cami is with you before, during, and after every session

1

Pre-Session Prep

Cami pulls your client's history, surfaces treatment approaches that worked with similar presentations, flags triggers from last session, and generates a suggested session agenda. You approve or modify before the client walks in.

2

During-Session Scribe

Real-time transcription with speaker labels and emotional tagging. Risk language is flagged immediately -- not after the session ends. Cami documents what was said. Cami never diagnoses.

3

Post-Session Processing

Cami generates your session note in any format (SOAP, DAP, BIRP, Progress), suggests homework and interventions, updates your treatment plan draft, and flags if progress has stalled. You review and finalize -- Cami never decides for you.

HIPAA

Built for HIPAA compliance

Clinical data demands the highest standard. Cami's clinical API is designed with HIPAA requirements in mind from the ground up.

  • No PHI stored -- audit logs contain only metadata. Zero patient data at rest.
  • BAA enforcement -- clinical API endpoints require a signed Business Associate Agreement before activation.
  • Full audit trail -- every clinical consultation generates a compliance log entry.
  • Audio deletion -- Scribe transcribes, then audio is deleted. Only text remains.
  • De-identification required -- patient context accepted only in de-identified form.
