Autonomous Embedded Guardian Rail

Every financial action flows through AEGR before execution. It's not a separate compliance layer -- it's woven into the command pipeline itself.

  • Pre-execution validation -- every command is checked against your configured limits and rail rules before anything moves.
  • Anomaly detection -- AEGR watches for unusual patterns, amounts, or destinations that deviate from your baseline.
  • Transparent logging -- every action, check, and decision is logged and visible to you in real time. No hidden steps.
  • Kill switch -- say "stop" or "cancel" at any point. AEGR halts immediately. You are always in control.

How Cami Money works

1

Connect your accounts

Say "Connect Plaid" and Cami walks you through secure account linking. Balances, transactions, and account details appear in your dashboard instantly.

2

Command by prompt

"Hold 500 USD." "Exchange to EUR." "What's my portfolio allocation?" Plain language in, structured financial action out. Cami understands the intent and executes.

3

AEGR validates every move

Before any financial action executes, the Guardian Rail checks it against your limits, flags anomalies, and confirms with you if anything looks off. Safety first, always.

4

See everything in real time

Rail status, account connections, transaction activity -- all visible in one place. You command, you see. No hidden steps, no black boxes.
