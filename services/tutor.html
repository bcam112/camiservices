Teaching, not telling

Most AI tools give you the answer immediately. Cami Tutor is built on a different principle: the learning happens in the thinking, not in the reading. Cami scaffolds first, answers second.

  • First response = guidance -- Cami asks a clarifying question, suggests an outline, or offers one piece of the puzzle. Not the whole solution.
  • Second ask = the answer -- say "just give me the answer" or "I'm ready" and Cami delivers the full response immediately. No gatekeeping.
  • Emotional awareness -- Human+ reads when you're frustrated, confused, or bored. If scaffolding isn't helping, Cami pivots to a more direct approach.
  • Session memory -- M.I.N. remembers your learning style, preferred pacing, and which subjects need more scaffolding. Every session is better than the last.

How a tutoring session works

1

Bring your question or assignment

Paste your homework problem, essay prompt, topic you're studying, or anything you're trying to learn. Cami works across every subject.

2

Cami scaffolds -- not solves

Instead of the full answer, Cami responds with a guiding question, an outline, a key concept, or a worked example of a similar (not identical) problem. You stay in the driver's seat.

3

You engage and Cami adapts

As you respond, Human+ reads your energy. Confused? More structure. Flowing? Less hand-holding. Frustrated? Cami pivots to a direct explanation. The pacing is yours.

4

Ask for the full answer anytime

Say "just tell me" or "give me the answer" and Cami delivers immediately. No judgment, no lock-out. The scaffolding is a tool, not a wall.
