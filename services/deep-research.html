Cami: Deep Research

One clear, balanced answer from many angles—so you get synthesis, not one model's bias or a wall of conflicting takes.

Context

Deep Research is for when the question is hard, the stakes are high, or you need more than a single model's take. Researchers, analysts, policymakers, and anyone weighing contentious or multi-stakeholder topics can use it to get a structured synthesis that surfaces disagreement and uncertainty instead of hiding it. Cami doesn't replace your judgment—it gives you a clearer picture so you can decide.

What Cami does for you

  • One clear, balanced answer—Cami compares multiple perspectives and surfaces where they disagree, so you see the full picture instead of a single take.
  • Save time on hard questions—policy, science, code, or debate-style topics get a structured synthesis, not a pile of links or contradictory answers.
  • Bias and uncertainty surfaced—when answers are one-sided or uncertain, Cami says so, so you can decide with clearer judgment.
  • Gets better at your kind of questions—over time Cami improves at synthesis and nuance on the topics you care about.

Use cases

  • Complex research questions (policy, science, philosophy)
  • Code review and debugging with multiple angles
  • Debate-style topics (e.g. open source vs proprietary)
  • Unbiased summaries of contentious or multi-stakeholder issues
