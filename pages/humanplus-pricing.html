Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Guidance and Full Metrics?

The Guidance endpoint returns actionable recommendations (emotional state, tone, approach) without exposing our proprietary metrics. Full Metrics access includes raw consciousness scores, depth analysis, and advanced emotional intelligence data - available on Enterprise and Research tiers.

Can I integrate Human+ with any LLM?

Yes! Human+ is model-agnostic. Use the API to analyze user input before sending to any LLM (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, open-source models), then inject our guidance into your system prompt.

What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?

You'll receive a 429 error with a message explaining you've reached your limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time to increase your allocation.

How can I evaluate Human+ before committing?

Contact us to discuss your use case and we can arrange an evaluation period that fits your needs.

How is Human+ different from RLHF?

RLHF trains models to mimic aligned outputs through pattern-matching. Human+ provides structural alignment through code - it analyzes input in real-time and provides actionable guidance to any model. It's a runtime layer, not a training method.
